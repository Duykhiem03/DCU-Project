The vibrant fashion of Nigeria!

The fashion of Nigerians are very unique! With many patterns and colours, you can never look dull. Normally when you roam around the streets of Nigeria, you'll spot men and young males wearing football jerseys, as the sport is loved by people over there. The women will wear skirts made out of colourful fabrics or wear simple trousers. However, when it comes to parties and events, Nigerians will bring out the traditional clothing out. The traditional clothing varies by the ethnic groups that exist in Nigeria. For example, the gele is a headscarf that is worn by the women. It is extremely common in Yoruba culture. The agbada is a long, embroidered robe worn by men, especially in the Yoruba and Hausa cultures. The eto-okuku is an Edo traditional attire, which is a wedding dress. It consists of the hair being tied up in a bun, and then coral beads are stitched into the hair to make a crown.
