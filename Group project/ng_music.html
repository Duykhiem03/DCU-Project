Nigerians are fun lovers!

Playing any music? Nigerians will be part of the crowd that begins to bust a move or two! Nigerians are fond of music, and created dance styles as well. What Nigerian artists tend to do is to make a song, along with a dance. A notable example is "Skelewu" by Davido. In general, Nigerians really love music, that is why many of them tend to learn instruments or enter the populated music industry to pursue their dreams. That fact makes me adore them even more.
