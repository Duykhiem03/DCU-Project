Tribes, Languages, Hairstyles

As already stated, Nigeria is a highly diverse place. With over 300 tribes, that means there are many languages that different people speak, and there is different customs and traditions they highly value. From those 300 tribes, there's a few that stand out from the rest - Yoruba, Igbo and Hausa. The Yoruba tribe speak Yoruba, and it's the most known and commonn tribe. Igbos speak Igbo and Hausas speak Hausa. In the Hausa culture, did you know they tend to cut their face, to result in tribal marks? Not only their face, but other body parts they deem fit enough. Some other cultures do the same, but it's rare. As you know, Africans love to express themselves no matter how they can do it. From tribal marks, music, dancing and even hairstyles, they can do it all. Examples of hairstyles from Nigeria are the Round about, Goddess braids, Alicia Keys (yes that is the name), Koroba etc. These pictures feature more hairstyles!
