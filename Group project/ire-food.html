Roast Dinner


Due to Ireland and England closeness in land and avaibnle food sources we have similar traditional meals, however still it is still believed that a Sunday Roast is an Irish dinner adn nothing can beat it. A Sunday Roast consists of one or multiple types of meat such as roast chicken, ham, lamb and turkey along with vegtables such as carrots, peas and green beans also normally has potatoes and gravy as well. This a staple in modren Irish life.

Sherpards spie is another Irish staple dish which is made of a potatoe top similar to the pasta for a lasanga. Then it is layered under with mince mized in peas and carrots.
