You should try some of these foods!

Nigerians love to cook, so it's to no one's surprise that there are many famous dishes from the country! Fun fact, did you know that the national dish of the country is Jollof rice? Not shocked, the meal is extremely popular worldwide. I'm very sure you've heard about it before. It is a delicious, orange rice, which can be quite spicy for those not accustomed to spices. It is usually served with a cold drink, like Pepsi or Fanta, and a piece of extremely well done chicken! It is a taste that one can't miss. Other foods that are quite popular are Fried rice, Pounded yam, Suya and many more. Fried rice is rice but a little yellow, and doesn't involve any spices. Normally, there's a lot of vegetables, seafood and meat sprinkled over it. It's delicious as well! Suya is just smoked, spiced meat served on a skewer. Pounded yam is considered a "solid", a food you eat with your hands, and is served with soups such as okro soup, egusi soup etc. Nigerian foods always liven up an event! There are also snacks such as Puff Puff, which is a fluffy treat with a crispy outside. It is really sweet and savoury.
